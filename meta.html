Tutors

The tutors are students who volunteered to help the school.

They have the responsibility to help the school on multiple points.

For exemple:

  • The examen
  • The piscines
  • The conferences
  • The partners' events
  • The 'She Loves To Code' events

They are rewarded with achievements, wallet points, log time and sometimes more. They also are the front line to meet the school's partners.

There are no restrictions to join. But you'll have to be a minimum active. After all it's a role that comes with responsibilities.

Only some of the tasks are limited depending on the progression in the cursus.

To become a tutor now you only need to contact a supervising tutor!

Supervising tutor

The supervising tutors are tutors with even more responsibilities.

Those responsibilities include:

  • Supervising the tutors and distribute their tasks.
  • To be an intermediary between the students and the bocal.
  • To organize discussion tables, parrainage events, make 19 teams for hackathons,...

To become a supervising tutor you'll need to prove your motivation as a tutor, be social and be present a lot!
